A Little Bit About Me

Hello everyone, I'm Alice Btushansky, living in Petah-Tikva. I recently graduated from the full military service as a First Sergeant. I am highly motivated and have excellent self-learning abilities. My service in the Air Force included responsibility for missile ballistic analysis in a combat classified technology unit. Being in this position required me to be very organized, pay attention to little details, be able to think quickly under pressure, and make the right decisions in a short amount of time.

Drawing is my passion, which led me to study UI/UX at "New Media" college. As a result, I gained a lot of skills in a wide variety of technologies and applications, such as Figma, Adobe Cloud, WIX, Canva, etc. In the future, I want to become a professional in this field. Additionally, I enjoy psychology, which is what led me to study it at the Open University.
